Coffee Drinks

Popular Coffee drinks
Drink Preparation Temperature Ingredients Details
Cappuccino Espresso Hot Equal amount of espresso and steamed milk Pour the espresso through the milk and serve
Latte Espresso Hot 2/3 or more steamed milk and espresso Serves best with cold milk with chocolate powder
Macchiato Espresso Hot Espresso with just a "mark" of steamed milk Add milk to the espresso after pressing.
Iced Coffee Drip or french press Cold Coffee poured over ice Can be sweetened with sugar or creme.
Frappe Strong instant coffee Cold instant coffee,water and milk Shake to create a foam,then pour over ice and serve.
Flat white Espresso Hot 1/3 espresso and 2/3 steamed milk A speciality in Australia and New Zealand.The foam is often decorated with motif.
